ABOUT THE PHOTOGRAPHER

Kayla Szymanski is a Senior at the University of Missouri- Columbia. She is a photojournalism major as well as a Textile and Apparel Management minor.

She is a photojournalism major as well as a Textile and Apparel Management minor. She has experience working as a community staff reporter and staff photojournalist at the Columbia Missourian and well as being a photojournalist intern at Kansas City Magazine for two summers.

Since a young age, she has had a passion for photography. She hopes her knowledge of photography and fashion will complement eachother in her future.

While Kayla currently lives in Columbia, after graduating she hopes to move to New York City to pursue a career in photography and fashion.
